How to Play: Arrange the tiles from 1 to 15 by clicking them to slide into the empty space. Click 'Shuffle' to start the game!

American puzzle author and mathematician Sam Loyd is often falsely credited with creating the puzzle; indeed, Loyd claimed from 1891 until his death in 1911 that he invented it. The puzzle was actually created around 1784 by Noyes Palmer Chapman, a postmaster in Canastota, New York.
